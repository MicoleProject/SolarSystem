The inner structure of Venus is simmilar to earth. Beneth a solid surface is a layer of melted rocks and in the center is an iron core.
The size of Venus is close to the size of earth.
Venus has a very thick gas atmosphere which mostly consists of carbon dioxide.
As result of the gas atmosphere Venus has a strong global warming which has increased its surface temperature to almost 500 degrees. 
Venus is rotating opposite to its circulation direction compared to the other planets.Venus rotates around its own axis in 243 days.
The surface of Venus is covered with 50 kilometers of clouds of sulphur acid which causes the planet to look yellow.
Venus is the most visible celestial body after the sun and the moon.
