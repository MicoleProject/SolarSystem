Uranus color is blue-green.
Its surface temperature is approximately -214 degrees celsius.
Uranus rotation axis is very tilted and that makes the planet seem to roll on its orbit around the sun.
Uranus has 27 moons which are very small.
As all gas planets Uranus has rings, more specifically 11 rings which are vertical.
Uranus is the third biggest planet in the solar system.
It rotates around its own axis in approximately 17 hours.
Uranus can easily be observed with a binocular.
