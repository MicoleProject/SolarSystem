Saturn is a gas planet with rings which consists of icy blocks of stone.
There are 7 rings of which the 3 largest is visible from earth.
The rings can be observed with a telescope and are the best distinguishing feature of the yellow Saturn.
Saturn is the second biggest planet in out solar system and it has 56 moons.
Saturn rotates around its own axis in 10 hours which together with its inner structure causes it to be very flat at ist poles.
