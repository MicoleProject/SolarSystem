Jupiter is the biggest planet in our solarsystem.
Jupiter is a gas planet and has no solid surface. 
All gas planets has rings and so has Jupiter. The rings are dark and very thin and they are only visible on pictures taken with satelite.
Jupiter is yellow and its most famous detail is the big red spot which is an enormous storm.
Jupiters rotates atround its own axis in ten hours. 
Today Jupiter has 63 known moons and new moons are discovered all the time.
